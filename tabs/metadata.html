SPROUT Metadata

This section outlines the standardized BIDS-inspired folder structure used in the SPROUT dataset. It includes raw audio, associated metadata, and multiple stages of audio processing pipelines (denoising, normalization, QC).

The task-specific audio recordings are named according to the following format:
> sub-[ParticipantID]_task-[TaskName]_acq-[AcquisitionType][_run-]_desc-rawdata.wav  
> sub-[ParticipantID]_task-[TaskName]_acq-[AcquisitionType][_run-]_desc-rawdata.json

Processed files in derivatives are named as:
> sub-[ParticipantID]_task-[TaskName]_acq-[AcquisitionType][_run-]_desc-denoisednorm.wav  
> sub-[ParticipantID]_task-[TaskName]_acq-[AcquisitionType][_run-]_desc-denoisednorm.json  
> sub-[ParticipantID]_task-[TaskName]_acq-[AcquisitionType][_run-]_desc-rawdataPreQC.json  
> sub-[ParticipantID]_task-[TaskName]_acq-[AcquisitionType][_run-]_desc-denoisednormPostQC.json  

Where [AcquisitionType] can be MicX, MicY, MicXY, or ZA.
