Pre-Processing

This section describes the pre-processing steps applied to the SPROUT dataset.

Audio Pre-processing

  • Noise reduction using spectral gating
  • Silence trimming at beginning and end of recordings
  • Volume normalization to -23 LUFS
  • Quality control metrics generation (SNR, clipping percentage, duration)

Quality Control

Each recording undergoes automated quality checks to ensure it meets the following criteria:

  • Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) > 15 dB
  • Clipping percentage < 1%
  • Minimum duration of 5 seconds

Detailed pre-processing documentation and interactive visualizations will be added in a future update.
