Pre-Processing Pipeline

Every recording goes through three stages—Denoising, Pre-QC and Post-QC—so downstream analysis runs on clean, validated speech.

1. Denoising

Spectral gating via noisereduce plus clipping prevention. 

# pseudocode
clean = reduce_noise(raw_audio)
clean = clip(clean, -1, 1)
save("output_nr.wav", clean)

2. Pre-Processing Quality Control

Check minimal metrics before moving on:

  • Duration ≥ 1s
  • SNR ≥ 15dB
  • Clipping ≤ 1%

3. Post-Processing Quality Control

Deep-dive metrics on the cleaned file:

  • LUFS
  • Spectral Centroid & Bandwidth
  • Pitch Mean
  • MFCC Variance
