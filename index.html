Overview of SPROUT

The SPROUT project collects speech samples from four-year-old children across diverse U.S. cities, aiming to build equitable AI tools for early childhood assessment.

Guided by FAIR principles, SPROUT prioritizes ethical protections, community engagement, and robust data governance.
